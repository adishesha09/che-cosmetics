Our Core Values

Cruelty-Free Commitment

At CHE COSMETICS, we firmly believe beauty should never come at the expense of animals. All our products are certified cruelty-free, and we never test on animals at any stage of production.

  • Ethical formulations
  • Ethical ingredient sourcing

Sustainable Beauty

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through sustainable practices at every level of our business operations.

  • Recyclable and biodegradable packaging
  • Carbon-neutral shipping options
  • Water-conscious formulations
  • Support for local environmental initiatives

Inclusivity First

Beauty comes in all shades, and we celebrate this diversity through our product range and brand ethos.

  • Diverse foundation shades
  • Products designed for all skin types
  • Accessible pricing without compromise

Community Impact

We believe in using our platform to create positive change in South Africa and beyond.

  • Local manufacturing supporting job creation
  • Beauty education programs

Our Quality Promise

Every CHE COSMETICS product is formulated with the highest quality ingredients, carefully selected for both performance and safety. Our team of cosmetic chemists works tirelessly to create innovative formulas that deliver exceptional results without compromising our ethical standards.

We maintain strict quality control measures throughout our production process, ensuring every product that reaches you meets our exacting standards. From material selection to final packaging, we monitor every step to guarantee the excellence you expect from CHE COSMETICS.
